Humidity

A Python script was used to visualize the weather of 500+ randomly chosen cities across the world, at varying distances from the equator. Out of the 601 randomly chosen cities, only 547 were used in the analysis. The remaining were eliminated due to incomplete data.

  • There are more representative cities in the northern hemisphere, due to the northern hemisphere having more landmass.
  • Generally, humidity levels (100% or less) greatly vary across the globe, though higher humidity levels are the majority (greater than 60%), especially in the latitudes between 30 and 70.
  • The northern hemisphere tends to be more humid than the southern.
