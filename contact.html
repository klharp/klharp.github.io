Quick Fact

The AIA has a policy on material transparency. The pronouncement states that the architect should be environmentally responsible and that knowledge of the life cycle impacts of building materials “ is integral to improving the craft, science, and art of architecture” because building materials impact the environment and human health “before, during, and after their use.”
